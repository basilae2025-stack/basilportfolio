Google Ads Case Studies

Performance‑driven Google Ads campaigns for lead generation, traffic and local visibility.

Case Study 1: Lead Generation Campaign

Campaign Overview

A 28‑day search campaign in the UAE focused on high‑intent lead generation. The goal was to drive qualified traffic, generate conversions and keep the cost per conversion controlled.

Strategy & Execution

  • High‑intent keyword targeting with segmented brand / non‑brand groups
  • Compelling ad copy and strong call‑to‑action focused on enquiries
  • Daily bid and budget optimization based on performance
  • Ongoing search term analysis and negative keyword refinement

Key Results

  • 2,500+ total clicks
  • Total spend around AED 6,310
  • Average CPC ≈ AED 2.53
  • 36+ tracked conversions at around AED 175 per lead

Key Takeaways

Consistent optimization of bids, keywords and ad copy delivered measurable conversions while protecting cost per lead, building a strong lead‑generation baseline for future scaling.

Case Study 2: Cost‑Efficient Traffic & Awareness

Campaign Overview

Traffic / awareness campaign designed to drive high‑volume website visits at a very low CPC while increasing brand visibility.

Strategy Implemented

  • Keyword‑focused search targeting with strong relevance
  • Continuous bid optimization for low CPC
  • Budget prioritized on the best‑performing keywords
  • Ongoing audience and search‑term refinement

Results

  • 1,190+ clicks generated
  • Total spend around AED 575
  • Average CPC ≈ AED 0.48
  • High traffic volume at minimal cost

Performance Insight

The campaign proved ideal for awareness and upper‑funnel traffic. With stronger conversion tracking and retargeting, this traffic can be turned into leads and sales in future iterations.

Case Study 3: Local Lead Generation Campaign

Campaign Overview

Local search campaign using Google Ads and location extensions to drive real‑world customer actions such as calls, website visits and direction requests.

Strategy Implemented

  • High‑intent local keyword targeting
  • Location‑focused bidding to prioritize nearby users
  • Use of all relevant extensions (call, location, site links)
  • Continuous CPC and ad copy optimization

Results

  • Ad spend around AED 1,044.58
  • 64.2K impressions and 1,080 clicks
  • 1,300+ local actions (directions, website visits, interactions)
  • 26 direct call clicks from high‑intent prospects

Business Impact

  • Increased local brand visibility in the target area
  • Generated direct customer enquiries and interactions
  • Strong intent‑driven engagement from nearby audiences
