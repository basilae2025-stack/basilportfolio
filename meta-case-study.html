Meta Ads Case Study

Three‑phase messaging funnel that turned awareness into cost‑efficient conversations for UAE tax services.

Funnel Overview

Starting with broad engagement, then shifting to optimized message campaigns and localized creatives, this Meta Ads funnel was designed to lower cost per conversation while scaling qualified enquiries.

Campaign 1: Engagement to Messaging Funnel

Objective

Generate messaging conversations from a broader engagement audience to increase inquiries.

Results

  • 84 messaging conversations started
  • AED 2,497.22 spent
  • AED 29.73 cost per messaging conversation

Strategy Used

  • Engagement campaign optimized toward messaging
  • Broad + interest-based targeting
  • Awareness-focused creative
  • Standard CTA encouraging direct inquiry

Analysis

This phase focused on scale and audience testing rather than cost efficiency. While the cost per conversation was higher, it helped build brand visibility, collect data for retargeting and identify the audience segments that responded best.

Campaign 2: Direct Message Objective (Optimized)

Objective

Generate cost‑efficient direct inquiries through optimized message ads.

Results

  • 40 messaging conversations started
  • AED 156.76 spent
  • AED 3.92 cost per result

Strategy Used

  • Messages campaign objective (conversion‑focused)
  • Refined audience targeting
  • Improved ad copy with clear CTA
  • Optimized creative format

Performance Insight

Cost per message dropped from AED 29.73 to AED 3.92 – around 87% improvement in cost efficiency by shifting from engagement to messages objective and tightening targeting and creatives.

Campaign 3: Localized & Seasonal Messaging Campaign

Objective

Generate high‑intent inquiries using culturally relevant and localized content.

Results

  • 26 messaging conversations started
  • AED 66.81 spent
  • AED 2.57 cost per result

Strategy Used

  • Arabic content and localized creative
  • Seasonal / occasion‑based messaging
  • Warm‑audience retargeting
  • Direct message optimization

Performance Highlight

Further reduced cost per message to AED 2.57 by combining localized creative with warm‑audience retargeting and strong direct‑response messaging.

Key Takeaways

  • Switching campaign objective from engagement to messages significantly reduced CPL.
  • Localized and culturally relevant content performed best in the UAE market.
  • Message‑focused campaigns outperformed engagement campaigns for lead generation.
  • Retargeting and warm‑audience segmentation improved overall cost efficiency.

Final Impact

Across all phases, the Meta Ads strategy generated over 150 direct conversations and reduced cost per message by more than 90% from the initial testing phase. The result is a scalable messaging framework that can be reused and adapted for future tax and accounting campaigns in the UAE market.
